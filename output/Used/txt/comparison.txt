======================================================================
KEY INSIGHTS & RECOMMENDATIONS
======================================================================

BEST MODEL FOR DEPLOYMENT: RNN (Simple Recurrent Neural Network)
- Reason: Highest accuracy (0.5461) and best F1-score (0.6592) among legitimate models
- Strong recall (0.8148) for detecting market "Up" movements
- Simpler architecture than LSTM - faster inference and training
- Good balance between precision and recall without degenerate behavior

ALTERNATIVE RECOMMENDATION: LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory)
- Close second with F1=0.6571, excellent recall (0.8519)
- Best for capturing complex temporal dependencies
- More parameters (642,977) but still computationally feasible
- Highest recall useful if prioritizing detection of upward movements

BEST LINEAR MODEL: Logistic Regression L1 (LASSO)
- Reason: Best accuracy (0.5486), precision (0.5745), and ROC-AUC (0.5342)
- Interpretable coefficients with feature selection
- Computationally efficient for production
- Only model with ROC-AUC > 0.50 (actual discrimination ability)

MODELS TO AVOID:
1. SVM (RBF): Degenerate behavior - predicts all "Up" despite high F1
2. Logistic L2: ROC-AUC below random (0.4888), overly regularized
3. CNN: Lowest accuracy (0.5116) among all models

OVERALL PERFORMANCE CONTEXT:
- All models operate near 50-55% accuracy (slightly better than random)
- S&P 500 direction prediction remains inherently difficult
- With standardized 7-day windows, RNN emerged as most balanced performer
- Neural networks (RNN, LSTM) show high recall but lower precision
- Logistic L1 provides best discrimination ability (ROC-AUC=0.5342)

PRECISION-RECALL TRADEOFF:
- High Recall (capture most "Up" days): LSTM (0.8519), RNN (0.8148)
- Balanced F1-Score: RNN (0.6592), LSTM (0.6571)
- Better Precision (fewer false alarms): Logistic L1 (0.5745)

ARCHITECTURE CONSIDERATIONS:
- Sequential Models (RNN, LSTM): Benefit from 7-day temporal patterns
- Feedforward (CNN/MLP): Simpler but less effective with this window size
- Linear (Logistic): Most interpretable, feature importance accessible
- Nonlinear SVM: Failed in this application due to degenerate collapse

Data Standardization:
- All models use identical data pipeline (basic_model_original_data.py)
- Features: 2,396 (2,380 stocks + 1 SPX Open + 6 day-of-week + 11 month dummies)

- RNN/LSTM process 7 timesteps with 2,396 features each
- Temporal dummies (day-of-week, month): NOT lagged (categorical reference)
- SPX Open: Included as predictor (lagged like stocks)
